Virtual Reality Projects

Kabusa Garden Homes's Interior

Albastic was commissioned to create a VR interior design experience for Kabusa Garden Homes. We created a virtual model of the apartment and integrated our real-time color changing technology, which allowed clients to instantly change the color of walls, floors, and furniture. Our designers provided a range of pre-designed color schemes and worked closely with clients to create custom looks. The project enhanced the client experience and facilitated collaboration between clients and Kabusa Garden Homes.

Albastic's Digital Human

Albastic has developed a cutting-edge VR project that features a digital human, which can be used to talk to clients and perform virtual movements. The project uses advanced motion capture and 3D modeling techniques to create a fully interactive experience for clients. The digital human can be programmed to perform a range of tasks and movements, such as giving presentations, providing product demos, and answering questions. This innovative technology provides a more engaging and interactive experience for clients, while also reducing the need for in-person meetings and consultations.
