American Corner Workshop

Albastic and American Corner collaborated to offer a series of workshops in their facility; teaching the basics of virtual reality and how to use the Oculus Quest. Attendees learned about VR technology, the Oculus Quest, and how to explore virtual worlds through hands-on exercises and demonstrations. The workshops were a success and participants left with a solid understanding of VR and the Oculus Quest.
